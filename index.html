links

If making links that open in a new tab with "target=_blank" use

rel="noopener noreferrer" too to avoid reverse tabnabbing

Bad example

a href="https://randomsite.com" target="_blank"

Good example

a href="https://randomsite.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"
