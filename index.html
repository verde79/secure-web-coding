links

When you want to make a link that opens the page in a new tab with "target=_blank" use rel="noopener noreferrer" too to avoid reverse tabnabbing

Bad example

a href="https://randomsite.com" target="_blank"

Good example

a href="https://randomsite.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"
